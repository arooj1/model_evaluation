PJM-APS

Allegheny Power Systems (APS) is zonal market under the PJM. This zone includes the EDCs of West Pennsylvania Power, Monongahela Power and Potomac Edison, across PA, OH and WV. Customers can save transmission peak related charges by responding to 5CP in either summer (June 1 - Sept. 30) or winter (Dec. 1 - March 31) depending on in which season the Transmission Zone Single Peak Happened.
